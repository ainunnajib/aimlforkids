What is NOAI?

The National Olympiad in Artificial Intelligence (NOAI) is Singapore's premier AI competition for students, organized by AI Singapore. It serves as the selection platform for students to represent Singapore at the International Olympiad in AI (IOAI).

Theory & Practice

NOAI tests both theoretical understanding and practical coding skills in AI and machine learning.

Competition Format

The competition includes written assessments on AI concepts and hands-on programming challenges.
